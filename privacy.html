Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-31

This Privacy Policy explains how Wipe & Recycle IT (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information submitted through wipe-recycle.com, including the equipment inquiry form.

Information we collect

  • Company name and contact name
  • Pickup city/state
  • Email address and phone number
  • Files you upload (such as inventory lists)
  • Optional notes you provide

How we use information

  • To respond to your inquiry and coordinate purchasing/pickup logistics
  • To evaluate inventory and provide pricing/offer details
  • To communicate status updates and follow-up questions
  • To maintain records for operational and business purposes

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed to operate the business (for example: email delivery providers, hosting providers, or logistics/shipping partners) and only for the purpose of completing your request.

File uploads and security

Uploaded files are used to evaluate your inventory. We take reasonable steps to protect submitted data using industry-standard security practices appropriate for a small business website. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; however, we work to minimize risk and limit access to authorized personnel.

Data sanitization statement

Our operational process is aligned with widely accepted guidance such as NIST SP 800-88 for media sanitization. This statement describes our internal handling approach and does not imply third-party certification.

Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request, conduct business, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Your choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your submitted information by emailing sales@wipe-recycle.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
